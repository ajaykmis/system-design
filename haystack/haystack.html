üéØ Key Learnings & Innovations

Metadata is the Enemy at Scale

The primary bottleneck wasn't storage capacity or bandwidth‚Äîit was the massive metadata overhead from billions of individual files. Traditional filesystems weren't designed for this scale.

Aggregate Small Objects

Instead of fighting the filesystem, Haystack aggregates hundreds of thousands of images into single large files, dramatically reducing metadata overhead.

In-Memory Indexing

By keeping a minimal in-memory index (using Google's sparse hash), Haystack can locate any image with approximately one disk operation.

Commodity Hardware + Smart Software

Rather than expensive storage appliances, Haystack uses commodity hardware with intelligent software design to achieve better performance at lower cost.

Write-Once, Read-Many Optimization

Photos are rarely modified after upload. Haystack optimizes for this pattern with append-only writes and read-optimized storage layout.
